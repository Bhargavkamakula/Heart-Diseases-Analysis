An interactive data visualization platform utilizing Flask and Tableau architectures to investigate clinical and behavioral dependencies in chronic heart disease diagnostics.

Clinical Context & Core Objectives

Chronic cardiovascular conditions represent complex clinical profiles influenced significantly by lifestyle behaviors and secondary pathologies. According to database research tracking tracking key historical populations, variables such as patient BMI metrics, smoking frequencies, and alcohol usage demonstrate notable co-occurrence intervals with underlying conditions like clinical diabetes and prior stroke histories.
